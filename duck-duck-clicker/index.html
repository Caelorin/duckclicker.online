What is Duck Duck Clicker?

Return to the classics with Duck Duck Clicker! This game is all about the pure satisfaction of clicking. Every click on the 2D duck gets you closer to unlocking new upgrades. It's a nostalgic trip back to the core of incremental games.

How to Play

The rules are simple: see the duck, click the duck. Use the points you earn to buy upgrades that increase your clicking power or generate points automatically. There's no complex strategy, just straightforward clicking fun.
